Poems in Boxes

Box 1

A gentle breeze, the sky so blue,
Whispering leaves, a morning dew.
Quiet moments, nature's grace,
Every day is a fresh embrace.

Box 2

In fields of green, where flowers bloom,
Amidst the sun, dispelling gloom.
A world of life, both near and far,
Nature's beauty, like a shining star.

Box 3

Stormy skies, a tempest's dance,
Chaos meets a wild expanse.
Yet through it all, life finds a way,
Resilience in night and day.

Box 4

Underneath a moonlit sky,
Dreams take flight, as time goes by.
Stars above, a cosmic sea,
Weaving tales of what could be.
